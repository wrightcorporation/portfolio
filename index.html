About Me

My name is Kody and I'm based in Madison, WI. Currently I'm enrolled in a Web Development Bootcamp. I feel really lucky that I get paid to do one of my biggest hobbies, and I have YWEB to thank for help starting my career.

I am a creative designer and developer, who aims to work with small businesses and marginalized communities to bring their passions to life. I offer both design and development services of web applications or websites.

I currently develop with a focus on the MERN stack, but still exploring other technologies and frameworks that catch my interest such as NextJS and Firebase! If you're looking for a developer to add to your team, I'd love to hear from you.
